2D Sandbox Game - Floating and Sodium Version

Elements

🖱️ Controls: Click & drag to draw. Scroll/Slider adjusts pen size.

⚖️ Physics: Everything falls by gravity. Objects float only if they are less dense than the fluid below them.

⚛️ Chemistry: Sodium reacts violently with water (explosion). Fire consumes wood and oil. Water extinguishes fire into steam.

🪨 Stone: Heavy and dense; sinks to the bottom.

🧪 Sodium: Less dense than water; floats, then explodes on contact.
